About Me

Hello! I worked in general and trade office administration from 2016 to 2024, where I gained valuable experience
in communication, organization, and problem-solving within a business environment.

In May 2024, I moved to the United States, taking the opportunity to reflect on my future career path.
It was in December 2024 that I discovered programming and quickly realized its potential. I was fascinated by how technology can solve problems and create new opportunities, which led me to pursue programming as my new career.

Currently, I am working at a restaurant while independently studying programming. I enjoy the process of learning new concepts and the sense of accomplishment that comes with overcoming challenges. I am passionate about continuous growth and always eager to expand my knowledge and skills.

My goal is to use technology to develop innovative solutions and contribute to building a better future.
